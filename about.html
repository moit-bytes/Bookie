As a student, I know how much we need to pay to buy a brand new book. For some students, they are economically stable to purchase whatever book they wish. But most of the students are not so well equipped for all the desired books on their own.
Now, take a scenario where you purchased a particular book, and then after your studying is done, that book is kept as it is dusting in the cupboard. On the other hand, the other student who needs that book struggles to purchase a new copy.
So, here I am with a web-based solution to solve this problem. Bookie provides the features to look for a book, borrow it or lend it to someone. Currently, the solution is limited to a city accepting the fact that the students can't go to other cities to collect books.
